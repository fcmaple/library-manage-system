"Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets" follows Harry's second year at Hogwarts. Mysterious attacks occur, petrifying students and stirring fears of a hidden chamber within the school. Harry discovers he can communicate with snakes, earning him suspicion from his peers. With the help of Ron and Hermione, he uncovers the chamber's secret and learns of its dark history involving the heir of Slytherin and a monstrous basilisk. Harry faces off against the memory of Tom Riddle, who has manipulated events to open the Chamber and revive his younger self, the infamous Lord Voldemort. Through courage and loyalty, Harry saves the day once again.
