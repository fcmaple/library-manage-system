
"Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone" is the enchanting debut novel of J.K. Rowling's beloved series. Orphaned Harry Potter discovers he is a wizard on his eleventh birthday and is invited to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. There, he befriends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, forming a formidable trio. They uncover a hidden plot surrounding the Philosopher's Stone, an artifact with the power to grant immortality. With the help of their wise headmaster, Albus Dumbledore, and facing challenges and dark magic, Harry and his friends must navigate dangerous obstacles, exposing the treacherous Severus Snape, to protect the Stone and thwart the return of the dark wizard, Lord Voldemort.
